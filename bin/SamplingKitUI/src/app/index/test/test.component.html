collection proces

Unlike some STIs such as Chlamydia (which can be tested from a urine sample), the HIV virus is blood-borne and therefore a small blood sample is the most accurate way to test for the infection.

Please order a kit only if you are happy with this collection process.

A safety lancet will use a small blade to pierce your finger (they are single-use, but we include spares). You will then need to carefully 'milk' your finger to collect around 10 drops of blood (roughly quarter of a teaspoon) into the tube we provide in the kit.
